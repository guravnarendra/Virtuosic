About the Event

The Debate Competition at Virtuosic 2K26 is designed to bring forward the power of ideas, critical thinking, and effective communication. Participants will engage in thought-provoking debates on contemporary topics across technology, society, and innovation. The event aims to enhance public speaking skills, logical reasoning, and confidence while encouraging healthy intellectual discussions. It provides a platform for students to express their perspectives, challenge viewpoints, and develop strong argumentative skills.

Competition Rules

01

Participation can be individual or in a team of 2 members.

02

Topics will be provided on the spot / prior to the event (as per rules).

03

Each participant/team will get fixed time for speaking (for/against topic).

04

In team participation, both members must contribute equally.

05

Use of offensive or inappropriate language will lead to disqualification.

06

Participants must maintain decorum and respect others' viewpoints.

07

Decision of judges will be final and binding.

08

Any form of malpractice or unfair means will lead to disqualification.

Judging Criteria

Content & Knowledge 20
Clarity of Thought 20
Confidence & Delivery 15
Relevance to Topic 15
Argument Strength 15
Team Coordination (for teams) 15
Total 100 Points
