About the Event

Hacksprint is a high-energy hackathon designed to challenge participants in creativity, coding, and real-world problem solving. With multiple tracks like Open Innovation, Web/Mobile App Development, and Generative AI, participants get the freedom to build impactful solutions. The event focuses on rapid development, teamwork, and innovation under time constraints. It provides an excellent platform for students to showcase their technical skills and bring their ideas to life. Get ready to compete, collaborate, and innovate at full speed.

Competition Rules

01

Prebuilt or pre-developed projects are strictly not allowed.

02

Participants must report to the venue on time.

03

Each team must have 1 to 4 members only.

04

Participants should bring their own laptops and required tools.

05

Teams must upload their project code to GitHub after completion.

06

Submission of the presentation (PPT) via provided form is mandatory.

07

Teams must stick to their selected track throughout the event.
