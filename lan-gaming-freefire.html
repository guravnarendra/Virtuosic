About the Event

LAN Gaming: Free Fire is an action-packed competitive event designed for gamers who thrive on teamwork, strategy, and survival skills. Players will compete in squads in a LAN-based setup ensuring smooth and fair gameplay. The number of rounds will depend on total team participation, and each round will feature a different map to test adaptability. Winners will be decided based on total accumulated points across all rounds. The more you fight, survive, and dominate — the higher your chances to win!
