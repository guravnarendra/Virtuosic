About the Event

Virtuosic 2K26 BGMI LAN Tournament provides a platform for players to showcase their gaming skills, teamwork, and strategic thinking. The event encourages participants to compete in intense battles, demonstrating real-time decision-making and coordination. It aims to foster competitive spirit, collaboration, and excellence in esports among students. Participants will get an opportunity to interact with skilled players and gain valuable competitive exposure. This tournament is designed to inspire future esports athletes and leaders in the gaming community.
