About the Event

As a highlight of Virtuosic 2K26, the Mini Militia Tactical Showdown transitions the spirit of technical excellence from the lab to the digital battlefield. This 4v4 Team Deathmatch tournament is designed to test team synchronization, strategic positioning, and split-second decision-making. In a high-stakes arena, teams must coordinate their loadouts and movements to outsmart and outgun their opponents. Whether you are a master of the sniper or a frontline rusher, your contribution is vital to your squad's victory.

Competition Rules

🏆

Tournament Format

▸

Game Mode: Team Deathmatch (TDM)

▸

Team Size: 4 Players vs. 4 Players

▸

Match Duration: 10 Minutes

▸

Map: Outpost

▸

Winning Criteria: Team with highest total score (kills) at the end of the timer wins

📜

Gameplay Rules

▸

No Custom Mods: Players must use the official version from Play Store/App Store. Use of Pro Packs or Mod APKs (Unlimited Ammo, Nitro, Speed hacks) → immediate disqualification.

▸
Weapon Restrictions:

✗ Prohibited: Saw Gun and Shield

✓ Allowed: All other standard spawns

🛠

Technical & Fair Play

▸

Connectivity: Disconnect within first 30 seconds → match restart. Disconnect after 1 minute → game continues unless organizers decide otherwise.

▸

Device Policy: All players must use mobile devices. No Emulators/PC play allowed.

▸

Spectator: One organizer will join as spectator or monitor screens to record the final score.

▸

Tie-Breaker: In case of a draw, a Golden Goal match (5 minutes) will be hosted.
