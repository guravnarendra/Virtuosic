About the Event

DIRECTO is an exciting Photography and Videography contest organized under Virtuosic, the Techfest of SSPM's College of Engineering, Kankavli. This event gives participants a platform to showcase their creativity, storytelling, and technical skills through visual media. Whether capturing a single powerful moment or crafting a compelling video, participants are encouraged to express their unique perspective. The contest emphasizes originality, creativity, and impactful presentation. It is perfect for students passionate about photography and videography.

Competition Rules

01

Participation is open to all students.

02

Individual participation only for photography.

03

Participants can register for Photography, Videography, or both (separate fees applicable).

04

Only one entry per participant per category is allowed.

05

Content must be original. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.

06

Submitted photo/video must be available on the participant's mobile device for verification.

07

Last date for submission: 8 April 2026 till 4:30 PM.
