About the Event

Auto Expo 2026 is a dynamic showcase event for bikers and car enthusiasts to present their vehicles with pride and passion. This is not a competition but a platform to display creativity, design, and uniqueness in automobiles. Participants can exhibit their bikes or cars, whether customized or stock, and share their passion with a like-minded audience. The event celebrates automotive culture, style, and innovation. It's all about showcasing your ride and making an impression.

Showcase Rules

01

02

03
