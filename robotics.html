About the Event

The Line Following Robot Competition is an exciting technical event where participants design and program autonomous robots to follow a predefined path. The challenge tests accuracy, speed, and control of the robot while navigating through twists and turns. Participants get an opportunity to showcase their robotics skills, problem-solving ability, and innovation. This event encourages hands-on learning and practical implementation of engineering concepts. It's a perfect platform for budding engineers to compete and innovate.

Competition Rules

01

Each team can have a maximum of 2 members.

02

The robot must be fully autonomous (no manual control allowed).

03

The robot should strictly follow the given track.

04

Any physical interference during the run will lead to disqualification.

05

Time taken and accuracy will be considered for scoring.

06

Teams must bring their own robot and components.

07

Judges' decision will be final and binding.

Judging Criteria

Track Accuracy 40
Completion Time 30
Stability & Control 20
Innovation / Design 10
Total 100 Points
