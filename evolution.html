About the Event

Evolution is a technical paper presentation competition designed to encourage students to showcase their research, creativity, and technical knowledge. Participants will present innovative ideas or research findings based on given topics. The event promotes analytical thinking, communication skills, and academic excellence. It also provides a platform to explore real-world problems through structured research. Students from different departments compete branch-wise to achieve top recognition.

Competition Rules

01

Each team must have a minimum of 2 authors.

02

Registration fee is ₹100 for up to 2 authors; ₹50 for each additional author (non-refundable).

03

Participants can select topics from the provided list or choose relevant topics independently.

04

Paper must follow IEEE format and be submitted in Word or PDF (LaTeX).

05

Paper must include: Abstract, Introduction, Methodology, Results & Discussion, Conclusion, References.

06

Abstract should not exceed 100 words and full paper should not exceed 5 pages.

07

Participants must bring one soft copy and one hard copy of the paper.

08

Each team gets 6 minutes for presentation and 2 minutes for Q&A.

09

Presentation must be in PowerPoint (PPT) format.

10

Judges' decision will be final.

11

Branch-wise winners will be declared.

12

Last date of submission: 6/04/2026
